Who Am I?

My name is Kieron, and I embrace being a neurodivergent individual with gratitude. Despite being on the spectrum and having ADHD, I am fortunate to maintain a fulfilling full-time job and navigate family life with a lot of help.

I am blessed to have an incredibly supportive wife and four wonderful children of our own, as well as four cherished Godchildren with loving and capable parents. Our families find solace and strength in our shared faith, with Jesus at the center of our lives.

Besides spending time with my family, my interests include motorbikes and experimenting with the Raspberry Pi 5.

Thank you for visiting my project.

If you like what I do, consider supporting me! Or drop me a message.
