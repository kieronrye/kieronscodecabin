Who Am I?

My name is Kieron, and I embrace being a neurodivergent individual with gratitude. Despite being
on the spectrum and having ADHD, I am fortunate to maintain a fulfilling full-time job
and navigate family life with a lot of help.

I am blessed to have an incredibly supportive wife and four wonderful children of our own, as well
as four cherished Godchildren with loving and capable parents. Our families find solace and
strength in our shared faith, with Jesus at the center of our lives.
